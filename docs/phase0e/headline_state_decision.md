# Phase 0E Headline-State Decision

## Ruling

HEADLINE-STATE RULING (ratified, binding): DEFER-WITH-STRUCTURE - no headline
state may be implemented in any phase until an explicit post-0E-06 decision by
Nickolis; component-first remains the architecture of record; Fresh/Stretched/
Vulnerable remain quoted legacy vocabulary only (not legitimized for evidence
reads, not killed for current public copy).

## REJECTED LEGACY VOCABULARY

The following terms are quoted legacy vocabulary, not evidence-read states:

- "Fresh"
- "Stretched"
- "Vulnerable"

## Rejected Evidence-Read Vocabulary

Rejected for evidence reads:

- any single team or pitcher state;
- any multi-input synthesized state without a single printable registered
  definition;
- anything availability, health, role, quality, depth, or pressure flavored;
- colors, tiers, grades, scores, and ranks.

## Candidate Family

The only candidate family after 0E-06 is single-axis, printed-threshold,
registered-definition descriptive states in the `outing_clean` mold.

Mandatory preconditions:

- inputs are PC-classified counted facts only;
- locked bands are forbidden inputs;
- diagnostics are forbidden inputs;
- completeness states are forbidden inputs;
- IO and EL-preconditioned types are forbidden inputs;
- thresholds are versioned;
- sample floors are printed;
- citations are present;
- UNKNOWN propagates;
- degraded variants are explicit.

## Post-0E-06 Decision Path

After 0E-06, Nickolis may open a scoped decision on whether the candidate family
should exist. Until that explicit decision, no product code, registry entry,
model field, enum, payload field, public label, or renderer may implement a
headline state.

## QUOTED LEGACY VOCABULARY

"Fresh", "Stretched", and "Vulnerable" are quoted legacy vocabulary only. This
decision neither legitimizes them for evidence reads nor kills their current
public-copy disposition.
